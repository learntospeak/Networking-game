Simple Explanation

What is happening

  • When you use a website, your browser sends a request.
  • The server responds with data.
  • Anything sent to your browser can be inspected.

Important boundaries

  • You only see your own data.
  • Other websites cannot access this data.
  • Seeing other users’ data indicates a vulnerability.
